About Imposter-GM: English Language Revision Tool

Overview
Imposter-GM is an interactive pedagogical resource designed to facilitate English Language and Literature revision. By using gamified mechanics, the platform assists students in mastering key curriculum objectives, including vocabulary acquisition, grammatical syntax, and literary analysis.

Learning Objectives
Lexical Development: Enhancing student engagement with complex tier-two and tier-three vocabulary.
Grammar Reinforcement: Real-time application of punctuation, spelling, and grammar (SPAG) rules through interactive challenges.
Critical Thinking: Encouraging students to apply contextual knowledge of English texts in a fast-paced environment.

Classroom Utility
This tool is intended for use as a formative assessment or a plenary activity to reinforce lesson content. It aligns with standard English National Curriculum (or your local equivalent) standards for secondary education.
